Welcome to Atlanta!

Atlanta is the capital city of the state of Georgia in the United States. It is a vibrant city known for its rich history, diverse culture, and thriving arts scene.

About Atlanta

Atlanta was founded in 1837 and has grown to become a major economic and cultural center. It is home to many Fortune 500 companies, including The Coca-Cola Company, Home Depot, and Delta Air Lines. The city offers a variety of attractions, from world-class museums and theaters to beautiful parks and sports arenas.

Attractions

Piedmont Park

Piedmont Park is a 189-acre urban park located in the heart of Atlanta. It offers beautiful walking trails, picnic areas, sports fields, and a dog park. The park is a popular destination for outdoor activities and hosts various events throughout the year.

The Georgia Aquarium

The Georgia Aquarium is one of the largest aquariums in the world. It is home to thousands of marine animals, including whales, dolphins, and sharks. Visitors can experience interactive exhibits, live shows, and behind-the-scenes tours.

Georgia Institute of Technology

The Georgia Institute of Technology, also known as Georgia Tech, is a top-ranked research university located in Atlanta. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in science, engineering, business, and more. The campus is known for its cutting-edge research facilities and strong emphasis on innovation.

Plan Your Visit

Whether you're interested in history, arts, sports, or nature, Atlanta has something for everyone. With its mild climate and abundance of attractions, it is a great destination for a weekend getaway or a longer vacation.
