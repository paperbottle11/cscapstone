Types of Fish that Live in Freshwater

  • Goldfish - The goldfish is one of the most popular freshwater fish. It is known for its vibrant colors and peaceful nature. Goldfish are hardy and can adapt to a variety of tank conditions. They are excellent pets for beginners.
  • Betta Fish - The betta fish, also known as Siamese fighting fish, is a vibrant and beautiful freshwater fish. It is known for its long fins and intense colors. Betta fish are popular pets due to their low-maintenance care requirements.
  • Guppy - Guppies are small and colorful freshwater fish. They are known for their vibrant colors and active nature. Guppies are popular among aquarium enthusiasts as they are easy to care for and breed.
