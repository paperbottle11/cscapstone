Sea Animals

Dolphin

The dolphin is a marine mammal known for its playful behavior and high intelligence. It belongs to the family Delphinidae and is found in oceans and seas all over the world.

Sea Turtle

Sea turtles are large marine reptiles that navigate the ocean with their powerful flippers. They are known for their long lifespan and ability to migrate long distances.

Whale

Whales are the largest animals on Earth. They are mammals that live in water and breathe air through blowholes located on top of their heads. There are many different species of whales, each with its own unique characteristics.

Shark

Sharks are a group of elasmobranch fish characterized by a cartilaginous skeleton, five to seven gill slits on the sides of the head, and pectoral fins that are not fused to the head. They are known for their sharp teeth and powerful jaws.
