Ultimate Frisbee Simulation

Welcome to the Ultimate Frisbee Simulation! This simulation allows you to experience the excitement and intensity of playing Ultimate Frisbee from the comfort of your own home.

Gameplay

In this simulation, you can control a player on a virtual Ultimate Frisbee field. Your goal is to score points by throwing the frisbee to your teammates in the endzone.

Use the arrow keys to control your player's movements. Press the spacebar to throw the frisbee. Be careful of the opponent's players, as they will try to intercept your throws and prevent you from scoring.

Features

  • Realistic physics simulation
  • Multiple difficulty levels
  • Dynamic AI opponents
