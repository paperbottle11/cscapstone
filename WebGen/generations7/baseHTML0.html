Ultimate Frisbee Guide

Ultimate Frisbee is a fast-paced and exciting team sport played with a flying disc. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to play ultimate frisbee:

1. Gather your team

To play ultimate frisbee, you need at least two teams with an equal number of players. Each team typically has 7 players on the field.

2. Set up the field

Mark the boundaries of the field using cones or other markers. The field should be rectangular with end zones at each end. The length of the field is usually around 70-100 yards.

3. Start the game

Decide which team will start with the disc. The game begins with a 'pull', where that team throws the disc as far down the field as possible. The receiving team then tries to catch the disc and advance it towards the opposing team's end zone.

4. Play the game

Once the disc is in play, the teams try to score points by passing the disc to their teammates in the opposing team's end zone. A player must throw the disc within 10 seconds and can't run while holding the disc.

5. Scoring

A team scores a point when one of their players catches the disc in the opposing team's end zone. The team with the most points at the end of the game wins.
